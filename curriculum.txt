Graph - a structure amounting to a set of objects in which pairs of the objects are in some sense "related". the objects are vertices and their relation are edges.

simple graph- Also called a strict graph. Its an unweighted, undirected graph containing no graph loops ormultiple edges.

3 fielding contraints: 

* client-server 
* stateless 
* uniform interface 

Sub-contraints of uniform interface:

* indentification o fresources 
* manipulation of resources through respresentative 
* self-descriptive messages 
* hypermedia
